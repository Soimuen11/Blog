If looking at her is inspiring,
then she attracts you.

If she reads you,
then you have a chance
at seeing importance
in the malicious spark
gleaming out of her 
greenish grey eyes.

But don't be in such a rush!
The flame enlightening her pupil
might fade out,
or disappear entirely!

So don't anticipate anything!
Let the flow of events drive you,
waiting for that intimate moment
when your fears fly away.

Only then will the fire 
burn with all its might.
This ardent desire consuming you,
will then be shared...
